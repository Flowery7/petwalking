Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our professional pet care services

What areas do you cover for dog walking and pet sitting?

We provide professional pet care across Huddersfield, Brighouse, Halifax, Holmfirth, Dewsbury, Elland, Sowerby Bridge, Meltham, Fenay Bridge, and surrounding areas including Wakefield, Leeds, and Manchester for our pet ambulance service.

Is your 24/7 pet ambulance truly available around the clock?

Yes! Our emergency pet transport service operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Whether it's 3 AM on Christmas morning or during a bank holiday, we're always ready to transport your pet to urgent veterinary care.

Are you insured and DBS checked?

Absolutely. We are fully insured and all team members have undergone enhanced DBS checks. Your pet's safety and your peace of mind are our highest priorities. We're also qualified in Animal First Aid and Veterinary Medicine.

What types of pets do you care for?

We primarily care for dogs and cats, but we also look after small animals including rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, and birds. Our pet ambulance service can transport any domestic pet requiring urgent veterinary attention.

How do I book a dog walking or pet sitting service?

Simply call us on 07931 869288, email pawsomepetcareltd@outlook.com, or message us via Facebook or WhatsApp. We'll discuss your pet's needs and arrange a convenient schedule. Same-day bookings may be available subject to availability.

What conditions can your pet ambulance handle?

Our pet ambulance is equipped to transport pets with epilepsy, disabilities, mobility issues, post-surgery requirements, and emergency conditions. We provide calm, professional care and work with PDSA, Donaldson's Vets, and Calder Vets.

Do you offer puppy visits and kitten care?

Yes! We offer multiple daily drop-in visits for puppies and kittens, including feeding, playtime, potty breaks, and medication administration if needed. Perfect for owners who work long hours or need occasional support.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies depending on service type, duration, and location. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote. We offer competitive rates for dog walking, pet sitting, and emergency ambulance transport. Discounts available for regular bookings.
