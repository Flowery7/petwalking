Welcome to
Paw-Some Pet Care

Reliable, friendly, and caring pet sitting for your furry friends!

We offer top-quality pet sitting services that give you peace of mind when you’re away. Our experienced team ensures your pets are well taken care of with love and care. We believe every pet deserves to be treated like family, whether it’s a daily walk to stretch their legs or an urgent trip to the vet. Our passion comes from the joy pets bring us, and we’re proud to offer a service that reflects the love and dedication we feel for them. That’s what drives us, and that’s why we’re here—to bring peace of mind to pet owners and happiness to every pet we meet.

Rest assured, we're fully insured and DBS checked for your peace of mind. Our qualifications speak for themselves:

Animal First Aid, Certified Veterinary Assistant, Diploma Diploma in Veterinary Medicine, Expertise in Dog Health, Breeds, and Anatomy Certified puppy and dog training Natural Remedies for Dog Illnesses and Dog Training Animal Science Dog Grooming and Parasite Control.



Who we work with

We’re also proud to work closely with the PDSA (we provide drop-offs at their Bradford branch) as well as Donaldson's Vets and Calder Vets, ensuring your pets get the best care when needed.

Available 24/7 to ensure your pets are happy, healthy, and cared for.
